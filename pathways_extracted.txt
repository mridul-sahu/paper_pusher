Pathways: Asynchronous Distributed Dataflow for ML
Barham et al., 2022

Abstract:
Modern ML models are scaling to thousands of accelerators. Pathways is a new distributed dataflow system that provides a unified, efficient abstraction for these models. It supports heterogeneous, multi-modal workloads and time-multiplexes accelerators efficiently.

Key findings:
- Pathways achieves ~100% efficiency at 2048 TPUv4 chips.
- It turns a datacenter into a single programmable supercomputer.
- Moves away from SPMD-only constraints.
